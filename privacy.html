Artist Vault — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

1. Information We Collect

Artist Vault collects only the minimum information necessary to provide the Service:

  • Telegram user data: Your Telegram user ID and username, used solely to identify your account within the Service.
  • Content data: Music release titles, dates, and content plans you create within the app, stored locally on your device.
  • TikTok data: If you connect your TikTok account, we access only the data you explicitly authorize, such as public analytics and trending hashtags. We do not store your TikTok credentials.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the Service
  • To verify your subscription status (Free or Pro)
  • To generate personalized content recommendations based on your music genre
  • To display TikTok analytics and trends relevant to your releases

3. Data Storage

Most data (your content plans, song releases, tasks) is stored locally on your device using browser localStorage. We store only your Telegram user ID and subscription status on our servers hosted on Railway.

4. Third-Party Services

Artist Vault integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Telegram: For user authentication and bot interaction. Subject to Telegram's Privacy Policy.
  • TikTok: For analytics and trend data (optional). Subject to TikTok's Privacy Policy.

5. TikTok Data Usage

When you connect your TikTok account, Artist Vault accesses only the data permissions you explicitly grant. We use this data solely to provide content recommendations and analytics within the Service. We do not sell, share, or use your TikTok data for advertising purposes.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We do not share your data with advertisers or marketing companies.

7. Data Retention

We retain your account data (Telegram ID and subscription status) for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

8. Your Rights

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for TikTok integration at any time

9. Children's Privacy

Artist Vault is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the Service.
